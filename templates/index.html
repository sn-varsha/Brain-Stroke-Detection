About NeuroDetect

NeuroDetect represents a groundbreaking advancement in medical AI technology, specifically designed for the detection and segmentation of brain strokes using deep learning algorithms on CT scan images. Our revolutionary system combines state-of-the-art convolutional neural networks with advanced image processing techniques to provide accurate, real-time stroke detection that can significantly improve patient outcomes.

This research project introduces novel methodologies in medical image analysis, achieving unprecedented accuracy rates in stroke detection while maintaining the speed required for clinical applications. The system is trained on an extensive dataset of brain CT scans and validated by medical professionals to ensure clinical relevance and reliability.
